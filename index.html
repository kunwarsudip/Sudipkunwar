　Hi, I'm Sudip Kunwar

Sudip Kunwar is a highly skilled and accomplished Chief Assistant Director with an extensive portfolio of work in the film industry.
With a remarkable career spanning 54 movies, Sudip has established himself as a trusted and integral part of the filmmaking process.
His passion for storytelling and dedication to his craft have made him an invaluable asset on various successful film projects.

Career

Sudip Kunwar began his journey in the film industry as a chief assistant director for the blockbuster movie "Nai Nabhannu La,"
and he continued to work on all subsequent movies in the popular series. This early experience allowed him to
hone his skills and gain valuable insights into the intricacies of filmmaking.
